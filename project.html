Projects


Here are a couple of my projects and assignments I have worked on. Take a look, ENJOY!




Feeling hungry?

Project that was created to help users find a recipe to cook based on their protein choice as well as offer a restaurant option if home cooking is not an option.

Day Planner

Planner that helps you keep track of your day and activites by color coding what is available and completed for the day. Inputs into the planner are saved in local storage.
